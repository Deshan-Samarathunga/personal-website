Lecture 6: Interview Preparation and Employer Expectations

This lecture helped me see interviews from the employer perspective. Employers evaluate technical ability, problem-solving, communication, attitude, teamwork, and cultural fit. Lecture 8: STAR Method and Mock Interview Practice

The STAR method taught me how to answer behavioral questions using Situation, Task, Action, and Result. It helps avoid unclear answers and shows real evidence of skills. I will use STAR to explain project work, teamwork, debugging, and problem-solving experiences, especially when describing backend issues and the steps I took to solve them.
